How it works

[Events] works in four simple steps.

1-Locate:

It's simple, on [Events] homepage, enter the name of the location you want to spend some good quality time with people
and click on search.

3-Go!

Select the [Events], wait for the scheduled day and go! Be prepared to meet new people, create new connections and have a great time!

2-Confirm or Create:

Go to check the events nearby the location you searched for and apply for any [Events] available. Every [gathering] has a
subject, that you can search for too! If no [Events] are available on the location you selected, you can create and share your event.

4-Have a good time:

Once you are at the [Events] location, look for the host, enjoy and have fun!
